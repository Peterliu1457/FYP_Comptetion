As technology progresses and electronic devices update constantly, the amount of e-waste grows at the same time. E-waste contains chemicals that are harmful to us and the environment. If not being safely treated, the environment will be severely polluted. Actually, these electronic devices can be reused through recycling and refurbishment. Not only can recycling help to lower the effect of pollution, but also help to raise public awareness and promote a sense of togetherness among citizens.

The reusable e-waste, after examination and refurbishment, will be donated or sold at a very low price to the needy. The e-waste not qualified for refurbishment will be organized and provided to licensed recyclers.

Recycling points are set up all over Hong Kong for the convenience of the public to donate their used computers and computer related equipment. We also provide onsite collection free of charge. Those who wish to donate may submit your request online or phone us. We will assign our logistics team to collect on an arranged date. Our logistics team is uniformed and will collect items according to the collection form. The exact amount of collected items is recorded on the collection form and will be signed by both sides after the amount is counted and confirmed. We will provide a Thank You Letter for donors on request.

On June 12, 2021, Caritas Computer Workshop successfully obtained Waste Disposal Licence (WDL) issued by the Environmental Protection Department. It is also the first NGO-non-profit organization to obtain this license. The computer workshop will provide you with more professional computer recycling and repair services to benefit more people.
